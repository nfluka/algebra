Tiramisu

Tiramisu is a coffe-flavoured Italian desert. It is made of ladyfingers dipped in coffe, layered with a wipped mixture of ges, sugar, and mascarpone cheese, flavoured with cocoa. The recepie has been adapted into many varaities of cakes and other desserts.

SASTOJCI:

  • 4 žumanjca
  • 100g šećera u prahu
  • 500g mascarpone sira
  • 250 ml slatkog vrhnja
  • 2 vća pakovanja piškota (48 kom)
  • 2,5 dcl kuhane jake kave
  • malo ruma
